About Me

My Background & Journey

I'm a passionate Full Stack Developer with over 7 years of experience building innovative web applications and exploring emerging technologies.

After completing my Computer Science degree at the University of Technology in 2018, I joined TechVision Labs where I honed my skills in modern JavaScript frameworks and cloud architecture. During my time there, I led the development of several high-traffic web applications and mentored junior developers.

In 2021, I transitioned to a Senior Developer role at AI Innovations, where I've been focused on integrating machine learning models into web applications and building developer tools that simplify complex workflows.

Beyond my professional work, I'm deeply interested in open source contributions, writing technical articles, and exploring the ethical implications of emerging technologies. When I'm not coding, you can find me hiking, playing chess, or experimenting with new recipes.
